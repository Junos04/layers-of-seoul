Layers of Seoul - My Perspective

Urban • Artistic • Landmarks

Mechanical Processes, Personal Meaning

This gallery brings together two mechanical processes: photography and coding. Photography captures moments as visual data, while code arranges and reveals them in a structured way. Although both processes are technical and precise, the experience they create is deeply personal. The images reflect the places, textures, and moods that shaped my time in Seoul, and the website becomes a way to reassemble those memories into a single narrative.
